Tiny Stroke Changes the Whole

The Whole contains the set of all possibilities. But by itself is static and without life.

The smallest dot creates a new area of interest in the Whole. It makes the page dynamic and creates vitality.

The blank sheet of paper represents wholeness. An unbroken, undivided whole.
